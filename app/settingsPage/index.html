About
This extension enables users to check out from CRM meetings across both Web CRM and Mobile CRM. Check-out details are securely recorded within the meeting record using custom fields.
Check-out can be performed from the Meetings list view or the Meeting details page. A valid check-in through Mobile CRM is required, and check-out is allowed only after the meeting end time.

Time Based Check-out Restriction

Users can enable or disable the time-based restriction for check-out.

This setting controls whether users are allowed to check out before the meeting end time.

Location Based Check-out Restriction

For optimal results, please ensure accurate location details are provided.

In some cases, location information may be approximate, which can affect distance-based validations.
